Emergency Medical RESCUE Of NYC

NYC's First Volunteer BLS-D First Response Unit

Segway Response Team
  

Emergency Medical RESCUE of New York City recently acquired several Segway HT's,
which are fully stocked with Basic Life Support equipment and defibrillators.
 The images below are from some of the events we have operated at since placing the Segways in service in July, 2008.
Feel free to contact us if you could use our services at your next special event.

This is the first utilization of medically equipped Segway HT units for emergency medical response in New York City.

EMR*NYC wishes to thank L.T. Lucent Tactical and Segway Of Utah for their help in equipping our new response Segways.

We would also like to thank Segway Of Annapolis and MTO Battery in helping us to keep the Segways in service.
